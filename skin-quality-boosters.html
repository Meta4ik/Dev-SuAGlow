  • What Are Skin Quality Boosters?

Skin quality boosters are advanced injectable or infusion-based aesthetic treatments designed to improve:

  • hydration
  • elasticity
  • skin texture
  • collagen support
  • skin barrier function
  • dermal integrity
  • overall skin appearance

Unlike traditional fillers that primarily create volume or contour, skin quality boosters focus on improving the skin itself.

K-Beauty clinics often combine multiple categories of boosters depending on what the skin actually needs. At SuA Glow, treatments are organized into four major Korean skin quality philosophies:
