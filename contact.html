Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need a referral to book?
No referral needed. You can book directly through our online portal or by calling us. New patients are always welcome — all consultations are included with your appointment.
What's the cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee. If you're feeling unwell, please reach out and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
How long do treatments take?
Most skin booster and injectable treatments run 30–60 minutes including consultation. First visits may take a bit longer as Sophia reviews your skin history and goals in detail.
Is there downtime after treatments?
Most K-Beauty treatments at SuA Glow are minimally invasive with little to no downtime. Some treatments like Rejuran may cause minor redness or pinpoint marks that resolve within 24–48 hours. Sophia will walk you through what to expect for your specific treatment.
Do you offer gift cards?
Yes! SuA Glow gift cards make the perfect gift for anyone looking to experience authentic K-Beauty. Call us or ask at your next visit to purchase one.
Not sure which treatment is right for me — can you help?
Absolutely. Every appointment starts with a full consultation with Sophia. She'll assess your skin, listen to your goals, and recommend the treatment that fits your skin type, concerns, and budget — no pressure, no upselling.
