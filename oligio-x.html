How Monopolar RF Works

The Science of Deep Tissue Remodeling

Dermal Thermal Penetration

Controlled 6.78MHz Energy targeting the Deep Dermis

Step 01

RF Current Delivery

High-frequency alternating current (6.78MHz) passes between the treatment tip and grounding pad, penetrating deep into dermal tissue.

Step 02

Molecular Friction

Tissue molecules oscillate millions of times per second, converting electrical energy to localized deep heat (40–60°C) within the dermis.

Step 03

Collagen Contraction

Immediate collagen denaturation and fiber contraction creates an instant tightening response, stimulating fibroblast activity.

Step 04

Long-Term Remodeling

Natural wound healing triggers sustained dermal remodeling and new collagen synthesis — results continue improving over 2–4 weeks post-treatment.

Oligio X FAQs

Is Oligio X RF Lifting painful?

Most patients experience a warming sensation during treatment. The GXG protocol is designed to maintain a manageable heat level — a 3 to 3.5 out of 10 discomfort rating is ideal for treatment efficacy. The built-in cooling system continuously protects the skin surface throughout the session.

When will I see results?

Some patients notice an immediate tightening effect. However, the most significant results — driven by new collagen formation — typically develop over 2 to 4 weeks post-treatment. Clinical studies have documented continued improvement at 4 and 24 weeks following treatment.

How many sessions are recommended?

Session recommendations are individualized based on skin condition, treatment goals, and clinical assessment. Some patients achieve their goals with a single session; others benefit from a series of treatments. Sophia will advise a personalized plan during your consultation.

What is the downtime?

Oligio X is a non-invasive treatment. Most patients experience mild redness or warmth immediately after, which typically resolves within a few hours. There is no significant downtime, though individual responses may vary based on skin sensitivity and energy levels used.

Can Oligio X be combined with other treatments?

Yes. Oligio X may be incorporated into a comprehensive skin rejuvenation plan alongside other treatments such as the SuA Glow Signature Treatment, skin quality boosters, or injectables. Combination protocols are always personalized at SuA Glow based on individual skin assessment and goals.
