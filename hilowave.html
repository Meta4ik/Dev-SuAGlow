Frequently Asked Questions

Is HiloWave an injectable?

HiloWave can be delivered via various methods. At SuA Glow, we often combine it with our ultrasound or microneedling technologies to ensure the most comfortable and effective experience for your skin type.

Can I do this before an event?

Absolutely. HiloWave is one of our most popular "Red Carpet" treatments because it provides immediate luminosity with zero downtime or redness.

Is it good for oily skin?

Yes. Dehydrated skin often over-produces oil to compensate for a lack of water. By restoring the water balance, HiloWave can actually help calm oily skin.
