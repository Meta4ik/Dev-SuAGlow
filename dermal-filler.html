Dermal Filler FAQs

How long do HA Fillers last?

Longevity depends on the product used and the area injected. Highly mobile areas (like lips) may metabolize filler in 6-9 months, while structural fillers (like cheeks or jawline) can last 12-24 months.

Will my lips look ducky or fake?

No. The "duck" look occurs from poor placement and over-filling. Our Korean-inspired lip technique focuses on enhancing the vertical height and natural shape of the lip, providing deep hydration and crisp borders, not forward protrusion.

Does it hurt?

We prioritize your comfort. We use strong prescription-grade topical numbing cream, and most fillers contain lidocaine within the syringe to numb the area as the product is placed.

What if I don't like the result?

Because we use exclusively Hyaluronic Acid (HA) fillers, the product is fully reversible. If necessary, we can inject an enzyme called hyaluronidase to safely and quickly dissolve the filler.
