Dermal fillers are injectable hyaluronic acid (HA)-based treatments designed to support:

  • structural balance

Numbing

Depending on the treatment area, topical numbing may be applied for approximately 15–30 minutes to improve comfort.

Many Restylane® fillers also contain lidocaine for additional comfort during treatment.

Treatment Time

Approximately 45–75 minutes depending on treatment areas and customization.

Patients may experience:

  • temporary swelling
  • redness
  • tenderness
  • bruising
  • injection-site sensitivity

These effects are generally temporary and commonly improve over several days depending on the treatment area and individual response.

Results

Results are typically visible immediately with continued settling over the following days to weeks depending on:

  • treatment area
  • filler selection
  • metabolism
  • movement patterns
  • individualized treatment approach

What is jawline filler?

Jawline filler refers to dermal filler placement designed to support lower-face structure, contour definition, and facial balance.

What is the difference between filler and Botox®?

Dermal fillers are commonly used to support facial structure, contour, and volume, while neuromodulators such as Botox® are designed to soften muscle movement and dynamic expression lines. Many patients combine both treatments as part of a comprehensive facial balancing approach.
