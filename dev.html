The main menu will be expanded from the current 4 core items to 8 primary landing points:

  • About Us: Dropdown (Story, K-Beauty, Sophia, Safety, Difference)

  • Referral Program: Direct Link
  • Promotions: Direct Link (Replacing "Specials")
