Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is HILO WAVE® an injectable?

HILO WAVE® can be delivered through several techniques depending on the treatment plan. At SuA Glow, we often combine it with ultrasound or microneedling technologies to ensure the most comfortable and effective delivery for your skin type.

2. Can I do this before an event?

Absolutely. HILO WAVE® is one of our most popular "Red Carpet" treatments because it provides immediate luminosity with little to no downtime.

3. Is it good for oily skin?

Yes. Dehydrated skin often produces excess oil to compensate for a lack of water. By restoring hydration balance, HILO WAVE® can help calm oily or combination skin.

4. How long do the results last?

Most patients notice improvements in hydration and skin quality for 3–6 months, depending on skin condition and treatment protocol.

5. How many treatments do I need?

Many patients benefit from a series of 2–3 treatments spaced about 4 weeks apart, followed by periodic maintenance treatments.

6. Does the treatment hurt?

Most patients find the treatment very comfortable. We use techniques designed to minimize discomfort, and topical numbing can be applied if needed.

7. Is there any downtime?

Downtime is minimal. Some patients may experience mild redness or small injection marks that typically resolve within a day.

8. What areas can be treated?

HILO WAVE® can be used on the face, under-eye area, neck, and other areas where hydration and skin quality need improvement.

9. Who is a good candidate for HILO WAVE®?

HILO WAVE® is well suited for patients experiencing dehydration, fine lines from dryness, dull complexion, or reduced skin firmness. It's also popular as a "glass skin" maintenance treatment for patients who want to preserve results between other procedures.

10. What makes HILO WAVE® different from fillers?

HILO WAVE® focuses on skin quality rather than structural volume. It improves hydration, elasticity, and radiance from within—creating natural-looking, healthy skin rather than altering facial shape.

11. When will I see results?

Many patients notice improved hydration and skin luminosity within a few days. Skin texture and elasticity may continue to improve over the following weeks as hydration and collagen support take effect.

12. Can HILO WAVE® be combined with other treatments?

Yes. HILO WAVE® is often combined with treatments such as microneedling, ultrasound facials, or other skin boosters to enhance overall skin quality and hydration.

13. Is HILO WAVE® safe for sensitive skin?

HILO WAVE® is designed to support hydration and skin barrier health, which can make it suitable for many patients with sensitive or dehydrated skin. A consultation at SuA Glow helps determine the best treatment approach for your skin type.
