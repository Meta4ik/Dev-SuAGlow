What Is Medical Tox?

Therapeutic Neuromodulator Treatments

Medical Tox refers to therapeutic neuromodulator treatments designed to temporarily relax targeted muscles or reduce overactive sweat gland activity. Common neuromodulators: Botox®, Xeomin®, Letybo®.

At SuA Glow, Medical Tox supports concerns involving TMJ/jaw clenching, teeth grinding, chronic muscle tension, migraine-related tension patterns, and hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating).

Medical Tox Treatment Areas

TMJ / Bruxism

Jaw Clenching & Teeth Grinding

One of the most commonly requested Medical Tox treatments. Neuromodulators may help relax overactive jaw muscles associated with jaw clenching, teeth grinding, jaw tension, and facial tightness.

Many patients seek treatment because they wake up with jaw soreness, grind teeth at night, experience chronic facial tension, or notice jaw muscle enlargement over time.

Chronic Migraine & Headache Support

Tension-Focused Muscle Relaxation

Medical Tox may be used as part of a broader management approach for chronic migraine or tension-related headache patterns.

Targeted areas may include: forehead, temples, scalp, neck, and upper shoulders.

Hyperhidrosis Treatments

Medical Tox may help reduce excessive sweating by temporarily decreasing overactive sweat gland signaling.

Underarm Hyperhidrosis

Targets excessive underarm sweating. The most commonly requested hyperhidrosis treatment.

Palmar Hyperhidrosis

Targets excessive hand sweating. Common among professionals, public speakers, healthcare workers, and performers.

Plantar Hyperhidrosis

Targets excessive foot sweating. Often requested for shoe discomfort and moisture-related irritation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Medical Tox the same as Cosmetic Tox?

Both use neuromodulators, but Medical Tox focuses on functional concerns such as muscle tension, sweating, jaw clenching, and headache-related tension patterns.

Can TMJ Tox slim the jawline?

Some patients may notice softening of enlarged jaw muscles over time as muscle activity decreases.

Does hyperhidrosis treatment stop sweating completely?

The goal is typically reduction — not complete elimination — of excessive sweating in targeted areas.

Can Medical Tox help jaw tension from stress?

Neuromodulators may help relax overactive jaw muscles associated with clenching and grinding in select patients.

Is Medical Tox painful?

Most patients tolerate treatment well. Depending on the area, topical numbing may be used for comfort.

How soon will I notice results?

Many patients notice early improvement within several days, with fuller results over approximately 1–2 weeks.

Can hyperhidrosis Botox help sweaty hands and underarms?

Medical Tox may help reduce excessive sweating in areas such as the underarms, hands, and feet by temporarily decreasing overactive sweat gland signaling.
