Seoul Man FAQs

What Is the Seoul Man Treatment?

The Seoul Man Treatment is a personalized Korean-inspired male aesthetic program. It focuses on enhancing masculine facial architecture (like jawlines), subtly softening signs of fatigue, and refining skin texture for a sharp, confident appearance.

Will People Know I Had Work Done?

The goal is subtlety. At SuA Glow, we prioritize undetectable enhancements. We want you to look like you’ve been sleeping better, working out, and taking care of yourself—not like you’ve had treatments.

Is Male Botox (Brotox) Different?

Yes. Men typically have stronger facial muscles and require different dosing and placement strategies to avoid "frozen" looks and to preserve masculine eyebrow positioning and expressions.

Can It Help With Shaving Irritation or Rough Skin?

Yes. The skin refinement portion of the Seoul Man program focuses heavily on texture and hydration. By improving barrier health and deeply hydrating the skin, many men experience reduced irritation and a much smoother overall texture.
