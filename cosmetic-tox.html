SkinTox

Micro-Dosing for Glass Skin

SkinTox is a Korean aesthetic micro-dosing technique using diluted neuromodulators injected superficially to improve skin texture and quality—without freezing muscle movement.

The goal: smoother texture, softer pore appearance, refined glow, less makeup settling, polished skin finish. Many patients describe it as “filtered skin in real life.”

SkinTox May Help:

  • Enlarged-looking pores
  • Oily skin
  • Fine superficial lines
  • Rough texture
  • Crepey skin appearance

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I look frozen?

Our approach prioritizes natural-looking movement and balanced refinement rather than aggressive freezing.

How long does Cosmetic Tox last?

Most treatments last approximately 3–4 months depending on treatment area and muscle activity.

What is SkinTox vs traditional Botox?

Traditional tox primarily targets larger muscle movement. SkinTox focuses on skin refinement, texture appearance, and controlled glow using superficial micro-dosing techniques.

Is jaw slimming popular in Korea?

Yes. Masseter tox for lower-face contour refinement is one of the most commonly requested Korean cosmetic tox treatments.

What is TrapTox?

TrapTox relaxes the trapezius muscles to soften shoulder contour and create a longer neck appearance.

What is CalfTox?

CalfTox uses neuromodulators to relax enlarged calf muscles in select patients seeking a softer, slimmer lower-leg contour appearance.

Can SkinTox be combined with other treatments?

Yes. SkinTox is frequently paired with Korean skin boosters, collagen-stimulating treatments, and skin regeneration treatments such as Oligio X.
