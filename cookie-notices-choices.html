What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help recognize your device and store information about your preferences, settings, and usage patterns.

Cookies may be:

  • First-party cookies, set by our website
  • Third-party cookies, set by external services we use (such as analytics or advertising providers)
